Before You Ask

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I book a place at an after-school club?

Visit our booking page, select your child's school, and fill out the registration form. Bookings are on a first-come, first-served basis.

What are the costs?

After-school club sessions are £15 per session, with term packages available. The science magazine digital edition is free. Print subscriptions are £24/year.

How do I subscribe to the magazine?

Visit our magazine page and choose between our free digital subscription or £24/year print + digital option.

Do you do science birthday parties?

Yes! We offer themed science birthday parties with experiments, lab coats, and certificates. Contact us via the form above selecting "Science Birthday Parties" for details.

How can my school partner with STEM Dorado?

We'd love to hear from you! Select "Bring STEM Dorado to My School" in the form above, and we'll arrange a call to discuss how we can work together.

Can I volunteer or work for STEM Dorado?

We're always looking for passionate science educators. Check our careers section or select "Careers & Volunteering" in the contact form.
