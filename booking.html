Booking FAQs

What's included in the fee?

The fee includes stationery and personal protection kits. All science materials and equipment are provided.

How do I pay?

An invoice will be sent after you submit the booking form. Registration is confirmed after payment of the full term fees.

Can I get a refund?

Please contact us at info@stemdorado.com for our refund and cancellation policy.

Can I book for multiple children?

Yes! Please submit a separate booking form for each child.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. We handle all personal data in accordance with GDPR and our privacy policy. Your information is only used for booking and safety purposes.
