About Our Clubs

Science Learning Has Never Been More Exciting!

STEM Dorado after-school science clubs operate in 5 primary schools across North London since September 2024. We are thrilled to extend our club to two more schools from September 2025.

Each week, children explore fascinating science topics through hands-on experiments, interactive activities, and guided discovery — all led by qualified science educators.

Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

When do clubs run?

Clubs run weekly after school hours, typically 3:30pm - 4:30pm. Exact times vary by school.

What ages are the clubs for?

Our clubs are designed for children ages 5-11 (Reception to Year 6).

How much does it cost?

Pricing varies by school and term length. Click on your school above for specific pricing.

Can you come to my child's school?

We're always looking to partner with new schools! Contact us to discuss bringing STEM Dorado to your school.
